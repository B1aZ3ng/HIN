An externality is when the production or consumption of a good or service has a positive/negative impact on 3rd parties who are not directly involved in this transaction, and is not reflected in the market price

# Positive Production Externality
Farmer keeps bees -> pollination helps nearby crops
Training workers → other firms benefit from skilled labour

# Negative Production Externality
deforestation -> bad for environment

Factory air pollution -> health of nearby residents

Airport -> both in a way positive = easier transport, negative = noise

# Positive Consumption Externality

Vaccinations reduce spread of disease
Education creates a more productive workforce
