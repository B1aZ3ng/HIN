# Energy in Electric fields
NOTE: Work Done = done by user on the field, NOT on the field towards the user
Potential Energy & Work Done
When you do work on an object against the field, potential energy of the object increases
Eg. if you lift something up, it increases GPE

Positive work = gaining potential energy, eg. moving a ball up
Negative work = losing PE, eg. dropping a ball and making it fall
### Attractive charges

To seperate charges, you need to apply a force against the direction of electric field
- you must do work and the system gains potential energy
### Repulsive charges
you must do work moving the charges towards each other, with the system gaining potential energy.
